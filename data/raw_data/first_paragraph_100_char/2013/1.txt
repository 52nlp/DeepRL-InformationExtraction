OAKLAND GUN VIOLENCE: Four people were fatally shot in Oakland, Calif., in separate incidents within a six-hour span starting Friday afternoon. The police chief vowed to intensify anti-violence measures. The city had 131 homicides in 2012, the most since 2006 when there were 148. The fatalities include Ken Harbin, 17; Larry Lovette, 30 and Eddiebo Rodriguez, 20.
