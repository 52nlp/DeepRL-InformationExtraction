Police are investigating an overnight shooting that left one person dead and four others wounded in Philadelphia's Feltonville section. Authorities were called to a home on the 4900 block of North Front Street shortly after midnight for a report of a shooting. The gunfire took place both inside and outside a home on the block, police said, but it wasn't known what sparked the shooting. Philadelphia police said a 20-year-old man was shot multiple times in his torso and was pronounced dead at Temple University Hospital at 12:36 a.m. He was not immediately identified. Four others were injured in the shooting; all were reported to be in stable condition this morning.
