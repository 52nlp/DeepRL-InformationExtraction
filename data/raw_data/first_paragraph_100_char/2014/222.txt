Westover Police vehicles are parked outside Doug’s Towing in Westover after three separate shooting incidents, including one at Doug’s Towing, left four people dead Monday. State Police say they’re seeking one suspect in all the shootings. Monongalia County 911 director Mike Wolfe said police are seeking Jody Lee Hunt, 39, of Westover. Police also are searching for a 2011 Ford F-150 extended-cab truck.
