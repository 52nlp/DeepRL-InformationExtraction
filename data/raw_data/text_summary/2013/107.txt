An early morning shooting in North Long Beach left one dead and two others in critical condition, according to a spokeswoman with the Long Beach Police Department. Long Beach Fire Department personnel transported the victims to local hospitals where Randy Chapman, 23, of Long Beach, who suffered a gunshot wound to the upper torso, was pronounced dead. Anyone who may have information regarding this crime is urged to contact Long Beach Homicide Detectives Teryl Hubert and Mark Bigel at 562-570-7244. Marlene Arrona, a spokeswoman for the Long Beach Police Department, said that the preliminary investigation indicates that the victims were outside of a residence when they were shot at multiple times. Two victims remain hospitalized in critical condition and another was treated and released.
