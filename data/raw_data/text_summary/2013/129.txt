AKRON, Ohio - Four people were found shot in the head in an Akron townhouse Thursday afternoon, police said. The Summit County Medical Examiner's Office conducted autopsies Friday that showed all four died from a single gunshot to the head. "My brother was a good kid; he ain't deserve to get killed like that. The medical examiner's office identified the victims as Ronald Roberts, 24; Kem Delaney, 23; Maria Nash, 19, and Kiana Welch, 19.
