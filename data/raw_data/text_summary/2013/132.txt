WARRENThe Associated PressGunfire erupted at a Federal Way apartment complex Sunday night where five people were shot to death, including a suspect who was shot by arriving officers, police said. Officers were using a metal detector to search a grassy bank across the street from the apartment complex this morning. The other man on the ground and a third man in the parking lot were found dead.
