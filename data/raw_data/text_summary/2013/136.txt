CHICAGO (CBS) — Seven men were wounded by gunfire on the South and West sides Monday, including four men who were shot by a lone gunman while they gathered on an Englewood porch. The man was taken in “stable” condition to Loyola University Medical Center, police said. An hour later on the West Side a man was shot in a drive-by shooting in the Austin neighborhood, police said. Both men were taken to Stroger where they were listed in “stable” condition, police said. All of the men were listed in “stable” condition police said.
