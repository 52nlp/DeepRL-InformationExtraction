OBERLIN, Ohio - Oberlin police tell NewsChannel5 that four people were shot in the parking lot of an apartment complex in the 500 block of West College Street around 11:30 p.m. Thursday. Police said that the four victims were able to run from the parking lot to a neighboring apartment complex and call for help. Police have one 24-year-old suspect in custody, who they have identified as Sean Woodings. Two victims, 17 and 23 years old, were struck in lower extremities and a 26-year-old was wounded on the arm. A 22-year-old victim suffered a grazing wound to the head.
