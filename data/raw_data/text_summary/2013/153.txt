CHARLOTTE, N.C Charlotte-Mecklenburg Police are looking for a gunman after a shooting early Saturday morning that left five people injured, including one person with life-threatening injuries. Three victims were taken to Carolinas Medical Center-Main, and two were taken to CMC-University. CMPD asks anyone who witnessed the shooting, or knows about it, to call their hotline at 704-432-TIPS and speak to a homicide detective. Treitley said the shooting happened after a disturbance among guests at a house party.
