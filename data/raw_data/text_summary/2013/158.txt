Smithfield police said 29-year-old Krystal Olymica David opened fire at the N & N Sports Bar and Grill around 1:30 a.m. Four people were shot at a Johnston County bar early Saturday morning, and police have arrested one woman in connection with the shooting. Police list the victims as: Elias Sanders, Charnise Jones, Cedric Mcerchrin and Felicia Jones. Mcerchrin is in fair condition, while Felicia Jones is listed in good condition.
