Boy, 17, slain in East OaklandA 17-year-old boy was killed and a man was wounded in a shooting in East Oakland, police said Monday. The teenager, Olajuwon Clayborn of Oakland, died, and the man was taken to Highland Hospital in Oakland with non-life-threatening wounds. Clayborn was a student at Castlemont High School in Oakland. The victims were standing on the 8600 block of Dowling Street when they were shot about 10 p.m. Sunday. He previously attended Berkeley High School.
