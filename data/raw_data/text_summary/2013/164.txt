OAKLAND -- A 17-year-old high school basketball player fatally wounded in a double shooting Sunday night in East Oakland has been identified as Olajuwon Clayborn, police said. Clayborn was a senior at Castlemont High School who transferred from Berkeley High this year, said Oakland Unified School District Spokesman Troy Flint. Police said Clayborn and a 22-year-old Oakland man were standing on the sidewalk when someone approached them on foot and shot them both. Another person at the shooting scene drove Clayborn to a hospital, where he was pronounced dead at 10:26 p.m. The man injured in the shooting was taken by ambulance to a hospital, where he was treated and released.
