“We got a call about 10 p.m. reporting shots fired,” police Chief Craig Foust said. Johnstown police continue to investigate shots fired Monday in the Hornerstown section of the city. It’s not clear if they were shooting at each other or were targeted by another person, police said. Four males were shot in the area of Horner and Messenger streets, police said. Foust said police questioned one of the shooting victims at the hospital Monday night.
