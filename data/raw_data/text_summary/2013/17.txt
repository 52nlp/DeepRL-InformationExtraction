TULSA - Tulsa police have identified the victims in Monday's five homicides in south and east Tulsa. Upon arrival officers found four women shot to death in an apartment and a 3-year-old child unharmed. Police say Williams appeared to have all his belongings so robbery may not have been the motive. Police say the man, identified as 52-year-old Christopher Odis Williams, was shot walking into his sister's apartment. Police have identified the women as Rebeika Powell and Kayetie Melchor, both 23, Misty Nunley, 33, and Julie Jackson, 55.
