Three people were found dead Friday morning from a shooting in Harbor Gateway. Friday's deaths were the first homicides in the Harbor Gateway this year, according to a Times' Homicide Report. A fourth victim was discovered more than a block away, near Del Amo Boulevard and Western Avenue, covered in blood, said LAPD Officer Christopher No. Los Angeles police received a 911 call about 5:35 a.m. from a resident reporting hearing eight or nine gunshots in an apartment building in the 1600 block of West 205th Street. Authorities said a red Dodge Charger and a gray Ford Explorer were seen speeding away from the scene.
