Four males, including three teenagers, were injured in East Germantown Saturday night in an apparent drive-by shooting. Someone in the vehicle began firing from the passenger side, striking victims in the shoulder, legs and buttocks. According to police, a car stopped at a stop sign at 21st Street and Medary Avenue around 8:30 p.m. They were taken to Albert Einstein Medical Center, where they were in stable condition late Sunday afternoon, police said. Police did not identify the wounded, said to be aged 15, 16, 17 and 20.
