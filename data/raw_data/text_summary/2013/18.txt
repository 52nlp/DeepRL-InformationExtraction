Barbara Fassett, 65, and her daughter, Elizabeth S. Fassett, 42, were found dead in their Chesterfield County home Monday. Dinwiddie County Sheriff's Department said after Herbert Bland Jr. allegedly shot and killed the two women, he went to his father's home in Dinwiddie County where he shot and killed him. Bland Jr. was seriously injured in the shooting and is currently being treated at VCU Medical Center for a gunshot wound. Chesterfield County Police obtained warrants for Bland's arrest Tuesday. When Dinwiddie County Sheriff's Deputies arrived just minutes later, they found him dead inside the residence.
