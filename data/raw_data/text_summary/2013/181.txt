Four people were rushed to the hospital last night after a shooting outside a Jersey City housing complex, police said. According to police, four people were shot at the complex and rushed to Jersey City Medical Center. The shooting occurred just before midnight last night at the Booker T. Washington Apartments on Fremont Street in Jersey City, police said. A Sheriff's Office K9 unit did recover a weapon at the scene, but authorities were unable to give further information on the weapon. Hudson County Sheriff Frank Schillari said that Sheriff's Officers were called to the scene to search for weapons.
