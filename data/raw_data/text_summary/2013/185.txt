Jeremiah Bean (Mug Shot From Lyon County Sheriff's Office)FERNLEY, Nev. (KRNV & MyNews4.com) -- Lyon County Sheriff's Office have identified all of the victims who were found dead this week. Those charges include Open Murder with the Use of a Deadly Weapon, Arson, Burglary, Robbery, Ex-Felon in Possession of a Firearm and Grand Larceny.
