Man wanted in Lunenburg quadruple shooting Posted: Sunday, May 19, 2013 11:02 AM EDT Updated: Wednesday, May 29, 2013 11:02 AM EDTThe Lunenburg County Sheriff's Office is searching for a man believed to be responsible for a quadruple shooting. Deputies say around 1:00 a.m. Saturday they responded to a home on Pleasant Oak Road and learned four adult males had been shot. Anyone with information as to the location of Codarrell Lee Yates or the incident at Pleasant Oak Road is urged to contact Lunenburg Crime Solvers at (434)696-3100 or the Lunenburg County Sheriff's Office at (434)696-4452. All four victims were taken to Community Memorial Health Center in South Hill and later transferred to VCU Medical Center in Richmond with life threatening injuries. As of Saturday the victims are listed in critical but stable condition.
