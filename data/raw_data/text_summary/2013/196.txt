A 19-year-old man was fatally shot in the South Side's Lake Meadows neighborhood in one of three shootings Monday night on the South and West Sides, officials said. Later, about 9:30 p.m., four teenagers were shot in 5200 block of West Iowa Street, in the West Side's South Austin neighborhood. Also on the West Side, a 34-year old man was grazed in the shoulder in the 300 block of South Western Avenue, Gaines said. A 19-year-old woman shot in the knee and a 17-year-old girl shot in the leg were both taken to Mount Sinai Hospital. Two of the injured, a 14-year-old boy with a graze wound and an 18-year-old woman shot in the arm, were taken to Loretto Hospital.
