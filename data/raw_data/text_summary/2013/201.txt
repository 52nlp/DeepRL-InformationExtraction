"The preliminary investigation indicates that Albert Peterson killed his wife and their children before taking his own life," the Fairfax County police said in a statement. A Virginia man is believed to have killed his wife and two sons in a murder-suicide at their home in Virginia, police said today. Today, Fairfax County police said that the investigation is now pointing toward Albert Peterson, 57, as the killer. All four members of the Peterson family received gun shot wounds to their upper bodies, according to a statement released by the police. Albert Peterson had worked for Northtrop Grumman until he resigned in 2009.
