FILED UNDERATLANTA--Three women and one man were shot just before midnight Friday night as they sat on the porch at their home in the 1000 block of Fair Street. Witnesses at the scene told 11Alive the shooting was probably the result of an ongoing feud that recently escalated on social-media. The four victims were transported to Grady Hospital with non-life-threatening injuries. No arrests have been made and the investigation continues. The shooter then fled the scene in the vehicle.
