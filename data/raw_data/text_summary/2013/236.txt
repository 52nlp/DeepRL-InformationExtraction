(Published Wednesday, June 12, 2013)The DuPage County Coroner has released the identies of the victims of a Darien triple murder/suicide. Homicides in the area are rare, but three members of the Kramer family were shot and killed in their Darien home in 2010. According to the coroner, Jeana Andrus, 48, died of a single gunshot wound to the face; Jessica Andrus, 22, died of a single close range gunshot wound to the face and Jennifer Andrus, 16, died of a single close range gunshot wound to the face. Law enforcement has released few details about what happened in the home, on the 500 block of Oldfield Road.
