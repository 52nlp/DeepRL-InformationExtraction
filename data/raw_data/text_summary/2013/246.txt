A man is dead and three other people were hospitalized Saturday after a shooting outside a southwest Houston night club, and police say the gunman remains at large. "The shooting started outside of the club and the shots were fired in the direction of the club," HPD Homicide Detective Jesus Sosa said. "Police said an altercation began when the two security guards who were shot refused to let a man enter the club. Investigators with the Houston Police Department say the shooting happened Manny's Place -- an after-hours club in a strip center in the 9700 block of Bissonnet near Country Creek. It's the second fatal shooting at a southwest Houston after-hours venue in the last week.
