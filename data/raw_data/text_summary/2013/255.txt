Six people were killed and dozens wounded in shootings across Chicago during a muggy first weekend of summer. On Saturday afternoon, a 20-year-old man was killed and two others were wounded in a shooting in the South Chicago neighborhood. Friday, the longest day of the year, was the deadliest, with three killed and at least 11 wounded in 10 shootings. Uriel Guadarramo, 36, who lived on the block, was pronounced dead and a 61-year-old was wounded. Ten people were wounded in at least eight shootings Sunday morning, and the violence continued Sunday night as two people were killed and another four were wounded.
