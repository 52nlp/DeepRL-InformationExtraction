PATERSON — A man who allegedly shot five people, one fatally, in one night last week has been identified – and is being sought by police on murder charges, authorities said. A 21-year-old woman from Paterson was also shot in the abdomen – but was treated and released from the hospital. An unnamed victim was walking down the street when Edge allegedly approached, fired multiple rounds, and struck the man once in the hand. About four hours later, Edge allegedly opened fire at a group of people near the intersection of East 22nd Street and Park Avenue.
