(Published Monday, July 1, 2013)Police released surveillance video of two men wanted for questioning in connection with the shooting of nine people at a Brooklyn house party over the weekend. Authorities say the surveillance video (above) shows the two men being described as persons of interest in the shooting fleeing the scene. Another two women wounded at the party later went to the hospital on their own. Emergency personnel took seven victims -- four men and three women -- to hospitals with gunshot wounds to their lower bodies.
