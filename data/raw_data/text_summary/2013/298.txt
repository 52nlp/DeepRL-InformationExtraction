CALAIS, Maine (NEWS CENTER) -- According to State Police one person is dead and three others are injured following a shooting. The Maine Attorney General's office has sent a team to investigate the shooting. A Calais police officer, John Preston, was also wounded. Police say Daniel Pinney was shot and killed by police after he shot and wounded a woman named Megan Sherrard and her baby at a home on Main Street. Both Preston and the baby were grazed by bullets and are expected to survive.
