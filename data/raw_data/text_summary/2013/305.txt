CHICAGO (STMW) — Six men were killed and at least 28 other people were wounded in shootings throughout the city so far this holiday weekend. The most recent murder was reported to police about 5:15 a.m. Friday in the Rogers Park neighborhood. Additionally, Chicago Police fatally shot a male in the Washington Park neighborhood on the South Side. A 27-year-old man was shot in the head and a 28-year-old man was shot in the leg. A 5-year-old boy was among the wounded — shot early Friday alongside two men at a West Pullman park.
