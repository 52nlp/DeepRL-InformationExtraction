LOS ANGELES (CBSLA.com) — The fatal shooting of a man was under investigation in South Los Angeles early Monday morning, the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department said. Anyone with information about the shooting should contact the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department’s Homicide Bureau at (323) 890-5500. The suspect vehicle was described as a light colored four-door sedan. “The suspect vehicle was driving erratically on 132nd Street when the occupant(s) suddenly fired multiple rounds at partygoers standing outside the location,” Deputy Mark Pope said. The shooting occurred around 12:20 a.m. at a party in a warehouse in the 13100 block of Broadway, officials said.
