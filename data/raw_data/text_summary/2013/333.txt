SAN FRANCISCO (CBS SF) — A man who was critically injured in a shooting in San Francisco’s Mission District early Friday morning has died, police and medical examiner’s officials said. He was struck in the upper body and abdomen by the gunfire and was taken to San Francisco General Hospital, where he succumbed to his injuries. Keith Smith, a 24-year-old San Francisco resident, was shot in the 3100 block of 16th Street just before 1:30 a.m. Friday, according to the medical examiner’s office. A 22-year-old man was also shot in the lower leg while a third victim suffered a graze wound to his calf.
