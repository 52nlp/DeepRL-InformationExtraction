Police are investigating a shooting at a birthday party in Hamilton Township, New Jersey that left one teenager dead and four others wounded. According to Mercer County First Assistant Prosecutor Angelo Onofri, a total of five teenagers were shot, one of them fatally at a house party in Hamilton Township. Police arrived to find 17-year-old Shakir Williams with multiple gunshot wounds. Police say a 17-year-old male who was transported to Capital Health Regional Medical Center with gunshot wounds to his torso, arms, legs and groin. Police say the teens were attending a birthday party for a 15-year-old resident of the home.
