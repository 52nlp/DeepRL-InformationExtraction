(CBS) – Officials say one person is dead and four others were injured after a shooting at a birthday party at a Hamilton Township, New Jersey home late Friday night. According to authorities, about 100 people were in the living room of the home celebrating a 15-year-old’s birthday party when gun shots were fired. The shooting happened around 11:30 p.m. on the 1100 block of Deutz Avenue.
