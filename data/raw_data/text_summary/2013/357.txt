Three teenage girls and a teenage boy were shot in Hartford early Friday morning when a family party end. One 19-year-old girl was shot in the elbow and the other girls suffered wounds to the neck. Police received a 911 call around 1 a.m. on Friday reporting that someone had been shot at Vibz Uptown nightclub. Minutes later, around five more rounds were fired from Main and Montville and two 19-year-old girls, a 17-year-old girl and an 18-year-old boy were shot, police said. Police determined that a fight broke out between a male and a group of males as the victims were leaving a family-organized party at the club.
