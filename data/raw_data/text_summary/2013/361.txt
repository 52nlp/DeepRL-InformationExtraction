× 1 dead, 3 injured in Far South Side shootingA man was killed and three other people were injured in an overnight shooting on Chicago’s Far South Side. The fourth victim, a 22 year-old man, took himself to Metro South Medical Hospital. A witness to the shooting said she tried to help the man who was killed.
