TOPEKA, Kan. (WIBW) -- Four people showed up to a Topeka hospital with gunshot wounds after a drive-by in southeast Topeka. Police identified the shooting victims as Tjuan Dean, 21; Demarcus Henderson, 20; and two males ages 16 and 17. Topeka police say the shooting was reported at 11:56 p.m. Wednesday at 221 SE Lawrence St. Last Wednesday, July 17, at about 9:50 p.m., a drive-by was reported in the same block, to 201 SE Lawrence St. No one was injured then. Soon after, four male gunshot victims arrived at a local hospital with non-life threatening wounds.
