Salinas police arrested a man Monday in connection with a shooting at a taco shop that left two people dead and five hurt. Arriving officers found a 22-year-old man dead of gunshot wounds. A 28-year-old man who also was shot several times was taken to a hospital, where he was pronounced dead. Police arrested Giovanni Pacheco, according to the Salinas Californian. Dave Shaw said the shooting occurred after two groups in the restaurant started brawling.
