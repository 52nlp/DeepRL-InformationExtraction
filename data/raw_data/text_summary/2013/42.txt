13, 2013 - Mardi Gras Shooting: Second suspect arrested in New Orleans' Bourbon Street attackFeb. 11, 2013 - Mardi Gras Shooting: Four people wounded on New Orleans' Bourbon Street during countdown celebration AP Photo/The Times-Picayune, Naomi Martin (AP) NEW ORLEANS - New Orleans police say a third man has turned himself in to face charges stemming from a shooting on Bourbon Street three days before Mardi Gras. The shooting injured four people, one seriously, during the run-up to Mardi Gras. Two Marrero, Louisiana men - 22-year-old Brandon Brown and 23-year-old Deron Bridgewater - turned themselves in earlier and were booked with the same charges.
