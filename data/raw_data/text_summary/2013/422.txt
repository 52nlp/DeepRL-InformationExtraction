Published: Monday, 8/19/2013Six injured in violent weekend in ToledoA violent weekend in Toledo sent six people to area hospitals with gunshot wounds and pushed the city's total number of victims for the year past 100. Including the six weekend victims, there have been a reported 104 people shot in Toledo this year, according to a database maintained by The Blade and based on police reports. At this time last year, 130 people had been shot in Toledo. Four people were wounded in a single incident in the city's Old West End neighborhood, which itself is generally insulated from such violence. Police were told that one victim was driven by a vehicle to a hospital before officers and rescue crews arrived.
