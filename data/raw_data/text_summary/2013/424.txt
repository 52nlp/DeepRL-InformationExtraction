Please enable Javascript to watch this videoST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) - A suspect is captured after returning to the scene of a shooting at a St. Louis gas station that left four people injured. As police were interviewing witnesses, the 27 year old suspect returned to the scene and was arrested. After an argument, the suspect got a gun from the car and shot the others. Police say it happened a little after 1:30am Sunday morning at a BP station at Compton and Olive. All the victims, ranging in age from 20 to 34, were taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.
