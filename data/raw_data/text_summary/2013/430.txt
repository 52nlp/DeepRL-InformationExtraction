By Trish WillifordChesterfield County investigators are searching for two suspects after five people were shot at a nightclub early Sunday morning. Family members said Jefferson Rex Rivers, the owner of The Twisted Rooster, was killed after a fight broke out at his club. Authorities told Eyewitness News the suspects' vehicle was spotted in Anson County off of Highway 109 south of Wadesboro. Crime scene detectives returned to the scene of the crime located on Center Point Road Sunday morning to collect evidence. They said their brother broke up the fight and then threw the people involved out of his club.
