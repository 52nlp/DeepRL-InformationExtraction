LAKE BUTLER, Fla. - Multiple people are dead or wounded after a shooting spree in north Florida on Saturday. At some point in the rampage, Allen shot former co-worker Lewis Mabrey Jr. while he was driving a farm tractor. Investigators from multiple agencies are working multiple crime scene and to determine a motive for the shooting. Union County Sheriff Jerry Whitehead told WCJB that the gunman, Hubert Allen Jr., walked into the trucking company where he used to work and shot David Griffis. He then encountered farmhand Rolando Gonzales Delgado, who Allen also shot and killed.
