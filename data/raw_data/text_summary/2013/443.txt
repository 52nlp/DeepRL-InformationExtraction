Click photo to enlarge «1234»OAKLAND -- Four people, including three children, were wounded Sunday in a daytime shooting near the corner of 104th Avenue and Graffian Street in East Oakland, police said. Officers were called about 2:20 p.m. to the 1300 block of 104th Avenue after receiving multiple phone calls about a shooting there, police said. Officers had portions of 104th Avenue and Graffian Street shut down for the investigation. A few houses were also hit by gunfire during the shooting, but no other injuries were reported. Police and Crime Stoppers of Oakland are offering up to $20,000 in reward money for information leading to the arrest of the shooters.
