Four people were shot at the Legion Club on Willis Street outside of Latta in Dillon County Saturday morning. If you have any information about the shooting , you're asked to call the Dillon County Sheriff's Office at (843) 774-1432. Cliff Arnette with the Dillon County Sheriff's Office. According to Dillon County Coroner Donnie Grimsley , 21-year-old Damien Simpson died from his injuries. Police charged Devante McNeil, 21, and Benjamin Bethea, 18, with murder in the death of Simpson, according to Arnette.
