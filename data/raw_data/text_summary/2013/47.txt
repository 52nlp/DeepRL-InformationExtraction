STILLWATER, Minn. (WCCO) — A judge found the man accused of killing a 9-year-old boy while randomly shooting at passing vehicles in Oakdale unfit to stand trial. Tran is accused of killing 9-year-old Devin Aryal as he rode in his mom’s minivan on February 11. During the shootings, Aryal was struck by gunfire while he rode in a minivan with his mother. He could still be found competent to stand trial at a later date. Additionally, a 68-year-old Oakdale woman, who was in a different vehicle, was also hit by gunfire.
