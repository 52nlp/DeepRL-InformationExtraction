(WMC-TV) - A 22-year-old man was killed, and four others were injured when several men reportedly started shooting at a Whitehaven apartment complex Wednesday evening. When officers arrived to the Birch Leaf Apartments, located at 1033 Whitaker Drive, around 8:30 p.m., one man was already dead. They say trouble at Birch Leaf Apartments, a very large complex, is rare. Investigators say several men, who were armed with weapons, opened fire and then may have fled the scene in a dark-colored SUV. Four others, who are men ranging in age from 21 to 25, were taken to the hospital with gunshot wounds.
