FILED UNDERWinston-Salem, NC-- Police are investigating an early morning shooting at the Nitty Gritty restaurant and nightclub on S. Main St. Saturday. When they arrived, officers found one person outside the club who had been shot and two other shooting victims inside the club. S. Main St. is blocked as part of the investigation an may remain so for some time. Anyone with information on this shooting should call Winston-Salem police at 336-773-7700 or Crime Stoppers at 336-727-2800. The three surviving victims were taken to Wake Forest Medical Center to be treated for their wounds.
