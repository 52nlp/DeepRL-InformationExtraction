WINSTON COUNTY, Alabama -- Four people have been found dead in a car in Winston County, according to Sheriff Rick Harris. The car was found on a logging road near a home in a rural area south of Double Springs. Forensics units and the Alabama Bureau of Investigation are on the scene, Harris said. Harris said the woman had seen the car as she was going to work Wednesday morning but hadn't thought anything of it. A woman called the sheriff's office around 8 p.m. Wednesday when she saw a suspicious car on the side of the road.
