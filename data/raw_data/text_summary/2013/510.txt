**{}Four people were found shot to death inside a vehicle in a remote area of Winston County, Sheriff Rick Harris confirmed to ABC 33/40 Wednesday night. UPDATE:According to Winston County Sheriff Rick Harris, it appears the fatal shootings occurred inside the white car, which has since been towed from the scene. Detectives and forensic investigators will continue its search throughout the vehicle for more evidence. A passerby noticed the vehicle off a logging road in the Black Pond community and notified the authorities. Deputies arrived at the location and found four bodies inside a car with an "out-of-state" tag.
