MARGATE (CBSMiami) – Friday, just before midnight in a quiet gated community in Margate, chaos erupted with gunfire. Any information on the incident, you’re asked to call Crime Stoppers at 954 972-7111. “It is, it’s pretty intense–scary too–concerned for my family as well because I don’t know who was shot or what happened,” said neighbor Patricia Santiago. Investigators are asking anyone that may know what took place at the scene to call Broward Crime Stoppers at (954) 493-TIPS. Crime Scene Investigators worked into the night, morning, and all day Saturday.
