(Published Sunday, Oct. 27, 2013)Police are investigating a fatal shooting at the Key Club Cabaret in New Haven, Conn., where one person was killed and five more were injured on Saturday morning. The shooting happened around 3:30 a.m. Saturday inside the strip club at 85 Saint John Street, at the corner of Hamilton Street. Six people were shot at the Key Club in New Haven early Saturday morning. During the investigation, police discovered drug paraphernalia and packing inside the club and said the venue reeked of marijuana. Jahad Brumsey, 29, from New Haven is listed in critical condition and three people are being treated for non-life threatening injuries.
