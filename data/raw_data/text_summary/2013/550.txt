(Published Monday, Oct. 28, 2013)One person was killed and as many as seven others injured in a mass shooting in Vallejo late Saturday night, police said today. According to a preliminary investigation, a group of young adults from South San Francisco were in four cars heading to Vallejo for a party, Vallejo police said. Police in Vallejo said a gunman opened fire on a group of people changing a flat tire, killing one man and injuring seven others last night. Police and paramedics responded to reports of a shooting and found five people suffering gunshot wounds. One of the neighbors in the area, who was a registered nurse, said she tried to help those injured.
