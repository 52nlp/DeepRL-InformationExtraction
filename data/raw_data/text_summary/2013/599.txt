One man was killed and three others were injured early Monday in a shooting at a Lake Street restaurant in Minneapolis. The Hennepin County medical examiner will release the dead man's name after an autopsy. One was pronounced dead at the scene, and two other men were taken to Hennepin County Medical Center. Police arrived at the restaurant, 1611 E. Lake St., and found three victims. Police reported that a suspect outside La Que Buena restaurant fired through a window into a group of people inside, striking four men, about 1:30 a.m. Monday.
