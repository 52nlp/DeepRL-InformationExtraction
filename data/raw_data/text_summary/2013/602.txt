VALDOSTA, Ga. (WTXL)-- Four people are shot early Saturday morning and police are searching for the suspects. We're told three of the victims have been released from the hospital, but Robinson was shot in the head and taken to a hospital in Tallahassee. Robinson was shot in the head and flown by helicopter to Tallahassee for further treatment but according to medical personnel, his injuries do not appear to be life-threatening. Valdosta Police say Ralph Robinson, Shekirrah Thomas, Demereay Doe and Bryant Turner were sitting in a car at the intersection of Gordon Street and North Ashley Street. Thomas, Doe, and Turner were treated and later released.
