At least one dead after shooting near Marshall University Posted: Friday, December 13, 2013 7:09 AM ESTHUNTINGTON (AP)- Police in Huntington are investigating a shooting incident that left several injured and one dead. The shootings occurred early Friday near the Marshall University campus. The report says he was killed by a bar worker. WOWK-TV reports that a man had been kicked out of a bar for a fight and returned with a gun. Huntington police confirmed the shootings and the death of one but could not immediately provide additional information.
