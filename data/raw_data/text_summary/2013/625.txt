TRENTON - A shooting in front of a South Ward convenience store last night left a 24-year-old city man dead and four people wounded, the Mercer County Prosecutor's Office said. Just before 10 p.m., a vehicle traveling on Home Avenue stopped directly in front of the store, a prosecutor's office spokeswoman said. Varlow was shot multiple times, the prosecutor's office said.
