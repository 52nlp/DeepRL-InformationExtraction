Joseph Brown (DCPI)A Brooklyn man has been arrested for shooting four people, at least one fatally, in a Coney Island apartment that also served as a day care center. According to the Daily News, Eva Natal runs a day center out of her apartment. Brown also allegedly shot 62-year-old Eva Natal in the stomach, and her other 40-year-old son (who he grazed in the head). Brown allegedly shot 24-year-old Matthew Natal, the son of apartment owner Eva Natal, in the head; he is listed in critical condition at Lutheran Hospital. Brown was arrested on Saturday, and has been charged with three counts of attempted murder and one count of homicide.
