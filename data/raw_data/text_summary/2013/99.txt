A 17-year-old has been charged in the death of a 15-year-old who was shot, along with four other people, at a birthday party over the weekend. Lt. Travis Anderson told WYFF that it happened during a birthday party on Tabor Street. Anderson said one of the shooting victims ran to the police station and told officers about what happened. Greenwood police arrested Dayterrius White at his home on West Crestville Avenue in Greenwood on Sunday night. White is charged with murder.
