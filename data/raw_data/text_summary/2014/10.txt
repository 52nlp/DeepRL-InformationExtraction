× 4 Wounded in South Los Angeles Shooting: LAPDFour people were wounded in a shooting in South Los Angeles on Tuesday evening, according to police. One person was shot in the leg, but the injuries of the other three victims were not known, No said. Police were called to scene in the 400 block of West 89th Street (map) for a report of people shots just before 6 p.m., according to Officer Christopher No of the Los Angeles Police Department’s Media Relations Section. It was not known if the shooting was gang related, he said. The shooter was last seen heading southbound on Flower Street, possibly in a burgundy Jeep, No said.
