Four juveniles were wounded in a shooting outside of a house party in Fairfield on Saturday night, police said today. Anyone with information about this crime is asked to call Fairfield police at (707) 428-7600, the 24-hour tip line at (707) 428-7345 or Crime Stoppers at (707) 644-7867. Officers arriving on the scene found four victims under 18 suffering from gunshot wounds, police said. All four were taken to area hospitals to be treated and each of the victims is listed in stable condition this morning, police said. An investigation into the shooting is ongoing.
