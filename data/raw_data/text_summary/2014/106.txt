(Photo: WUSA)WASHINGTON (WUSA9) -- An early morning shooting in southeast D.C. sent four people to the hospital with various injuries. Once officers arrived at the scene they found three men and one woman suffering from gun shot wounds to their arms and legs. Two suspects are described as black males, wearing ski masks and armed with handguns. The fourth and fifth suspects are only described as black males. Police received a report of a shooting in the 1500 block of 19th Street just after midnight on Monday.
