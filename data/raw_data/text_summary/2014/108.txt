Police were dispatched around 1:53 a.m. Sunday to Kato Entertainment Center — Kato Ballroom — on 200 E. Chestnut St., on a report of gunshots. MINNEAPOLIS (WCCO) — An overnight shooting in Mankato, Minn. left four people shot, including one person who died from their injuries, and three arrested, according to authorities. According to Kato Ballroom owner Tania Cordes, the center rented the back banquet room to the South Sudanese Group at Minnesota State University for a celebratory party. Officers say one person was arrested and a handgun was recovered at the scene. “As they were within a block, police could hear some of the gun shots.” Mankato Public Safety Commander Jeremy Clifton said.
