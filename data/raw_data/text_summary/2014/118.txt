Four people were injured in a shooting in the city's River North neighborhood early Sunday morning. Police said a 21-year-old man was shot in the arm, a 20-year-old man was shot in the right foot and a 19-year-old man was shot in the left shoulder. Police said a 20-year-old man was shot in the head. Chicago police originally said at the scene that one of the victims had died, but police corrected the statement and said all of the victims are alive. All three were transported to local hospitals in stable condition, according to police.
