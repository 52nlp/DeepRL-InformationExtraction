DeKalb County police are looking for at least two more young men, after a home invasion over the weekend left a baby dead and three women in the hospital. Nine-month-old Kendarius Edwards died in the shooting at Tolani Farm Road in Stone Mountain. “My sister called me crying she was shot and her grandbaby was dead,” said the victim’s aunt, Wanda Smith. Police said this latest shooting may be connected to six others, including the one at a party on May 3.
