Correction: Due to incorrect information supplied by police, an earlier version of this story incorrectly said the shooting occurred outside the Stadium Club. Three men and two women were shot and wounded early Saturday in Northeast Washington, according to D.C. police. Officer Hugh Carew, a D.C. police spokesman, said the group was outside when a suspect began shooting at them. Police said the shooting occurred about 2:55 a.m. near the corner of Queens Chapel Road and Adams Place NE, in an area that is home to several clubs. The victims were taken to a local hospital with serious injuries, he saidPolice did not have a description of the shooter.
