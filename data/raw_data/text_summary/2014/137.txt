A 15-year-old boy was killed and seven people were injured in a shooting late Friday night at a graduation party in Gentilly, New Orleans police said. The 15-year-old boy suffered a gunshot wound and died at a hospital, police said. Three suffered non-life-threatening injuries. Three men, in ages ranging from 22 to 25, suffered non-life-threatening injuries.
