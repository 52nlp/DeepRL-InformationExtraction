Drive-by shooting in Paterson leaves six people woundedBy JOE MALINCONICOPaterson PressPATERSON – Six people who were hanging around outside a liquor store were wounded Monday night during a drive-by shooting in a high-crime area of the city’s 4th Ward, police said. After the shooting, the vehicle sped west on Godwin towards Carroll Street, police said. A 45-year-old woman was wounded in the foot, an 18-year-old man in the shoulder and a 17-year-old boy was grazed in the head, police said. Authorities say none of the victims – four men and two women – suffered life-threatening wounds.
