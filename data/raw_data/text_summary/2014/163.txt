The Sonoma County Sheriff’s Office responded at 12:47 a.m. to a residence on Windsor Avenue in response to a report of shots fired, according to the Sonoma County Sheriff’s Office. WINDSOR (CBS SF) — A shooting Sunday morning in Windsor left a teenager dead and three others injured, deputies said. Anyone with information on the shooting is asked to call the Sonoma County Sheriff’s Office Violent Crimes Unit at (707) 565-2185. Three other victims were also suffering from gunshot wounds with two shot in the leg and one shot in the hand. Deputies arrived on the scene to find a teenage boy had been shot in the chest.
