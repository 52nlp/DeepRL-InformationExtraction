Chester police said five people were hospitalized, including two teens, following shootings in the 1400 block of West Congress Street early Wednesday morning. Verlondon Gary, 32, of Chester, was dead at the scene; two city women were wounded, one critically. The five victims, including the teens, were caught in apparent crossfire. Investigations are ongoing by the Chester Police Department and the countys Criminal Investigation Division. We are looking to see if the shooting incidents are connected, Delaware County District Attorney Jack Whelan said Wednesday night.
