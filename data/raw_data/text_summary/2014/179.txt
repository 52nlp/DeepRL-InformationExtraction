A 26-year-old man has died following a shooting outside a Southeast Portland strip club early Saturday. Officers and emergency medical personnel were called to Soobie's Bar & Strip Club, 333 S.E. Police said initial indications are that it was a gang-related shooting in the strip club's parking lot. They ask that anyone with information about the shooting call the police non-emergency line, 503-823-3333. Police say it was a "gang-related" incident that left four others shot.
