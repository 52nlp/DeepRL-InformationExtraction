Kentucky State Police are working to figure out how the four victims were shot early this morning in the Stopover Community of Pike County. Police say they received a 911 call around 4:00 Monday morning about a shooting at a trailer. Police at the KSP post in Pikeville aren't releasing many details about the shooting. A shooting at an Eastern Kentucky home sent four people to the hospital. Three men and one woman were airlifted to Pikeville Medical Center.
