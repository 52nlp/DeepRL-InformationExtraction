One dead, three injured in drive-by shootingOne person is dead and three others are injured after a suspect shot into an occupied car early Sunday morning, Las Vegas Metro Police said. Anyone with information is urged to call the Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department Homicide Section at 702-828-3521 or email at homicide@lvmpd.com. One of the victims was pronounced dead at the scene while three others were taken to University Medical Center for treatment, police said. The suspects drove off and the victims drove to the convenience store to call police. To remain anonymous, call Crime Stoppers at 702-385-5555 or visit www.crimestoppersofnv.com.
