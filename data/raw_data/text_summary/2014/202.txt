NORTH BRADDOCK (KDKA) — Police are investigating a shooting that left multiple people wounded Wednesday afternoon in North Braddock. Meanwhile, Allegheny County Police believe a report of shots fired Wednesday evening in Rankin is connected to the North Braddock shooting as well. Multiple police departments were also on scene into the evening collecting evidence, including North Braddock, East Pittsburgh and Allegheny County Police.
