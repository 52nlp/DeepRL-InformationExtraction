Smith family (Photo: NEWS CENTER)SACO, Maine (AP) -- Funerals are scheduled for Monday in Arizona for a mother and her children who were killed in Maine. Police say Joel Smith killed his family before taking his own life. The services are being held in Phoenix for Heather Smith, along with 12-year-old Jason Montez and 7-year-old Noah Montez and 4-year-old Lily. Family and friends say the parents were having financial struggles and dealing with addiction. They were shot to death on July 26 in Saco, Maine.
