× Beloit police: One dead, three injured after shooting incident in Summit Park SaturdayBELOIT (WITI) — City of Beloit police say one person has died after being shot in a park on Saturday evening, August 2nd. City of Beloit police were called out to Summit Park on Henry Avenue in the City of Beloit shortly before 9:00 p.m. Saturday. Beloit Police Evidence Technicians were on scene at Summit Park following this shooting incident. Police say a third subject was injured in the incident. Police say a fourth victim — a female, arrived at the hospital with a non life-threatening gunshot wound that is believed to have also occurred during the incident.
