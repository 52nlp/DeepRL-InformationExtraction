× Violent Friday night, Saturday morning in Milwaukee: Six shot in three separate incidentsMILWAUKEE (WITI) — Milwaukee police are investigating three separate shooting incidents that occurred late Friday night, August 8th and early Saturday morning, August 9th. The first shooting incident occurred in the 2700 block of N. 23rd Street (23rd and Center) around 10:52 p.m. According to police, a 27-year-old Milwaukee man suffered a non life-threatening gunshot wound during an ongoing neighborhood dispute. According to police, a 39-year-old West Allis woman suffered a single, non life-threatening gunshot wound during an armed robbery. The third shooting incident occurred in the 4000 block of N. Fond du Lac Avenue (40th and Fond du Lac) around 1:10 a.m.
