MINNEAPOLIS (WCCO) — A shooting inside a downtown Minneapolis nightclub early Saturday morning sent nine people to the hospital, including a member of the Minnesota Vikings. Minneapolis Police say the shooting happened at about 1:40 a.m. at 400 Soundbar, located on the 400 block of 3rd Avenue North. Minneapolis Police Assistant Chief Matt Clark says the shooting was not random, and the suspect was targeting one person.
