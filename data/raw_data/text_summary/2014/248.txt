EAST PALO ALTO - Police are investigating a Sunday night shooting in East Palo Alto that killed one person and sent four people to the hospital. The slain man was later identified as Nazario Barajas, 18, of East Palo Alto. Fire officials said the East Palo Alto Police Department is continuing to investigate the shooting.
