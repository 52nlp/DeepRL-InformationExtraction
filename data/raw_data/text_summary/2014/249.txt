View Full Caption Getty Images/File PhotoCHICAGO — A 30-year-old man was fatally shot and three others were injured during a drive-by shooting at an Auburn Gresham lounge early Sunday. Four men were in a lounge early Sunday when a passing car fired into the building. Police had been called to the club before the shooting — at 1 a.m. and 2:10 a.m. — for noise, the Tribune reported. A 39-year-old man was also taken to Christ Hospital in stable condition after being shot in his buttocks. A 32-year-old man was shot in his chest and taken to Stroger in serious condition.
