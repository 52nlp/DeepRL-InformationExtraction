An 18-year-old woman and a 21-year-old Highland man were listed in moderate to critical condition. Five people were injured, two of them seriously, early Tuesday when a man opened fire inside a Highland garage, authorities said. Three other women, ages 21, 23 and 24, were listed in stable condition. Callers wishing to remain anonymous were urged to contact the We-tip Hotline at (800) 78-CRIME (782-7463) or online at www.wetip.com. The victims were inside the garage of an apartment complex in the 25000 block of 5th Street when an unidentified man walked up to the group at around midnight and sprayed them with bullets, authorities said.
