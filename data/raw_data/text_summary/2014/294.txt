A child and three adults are dead after an apparent murder-suicide shooting in South Richmond, according to police. Police said inside the home, they found three shooting victims: a toddler, an adult woman over 18, and an adult man with what police said was a self-inflicted gunshot wound. Police are confident that the male victim's gunshot wound was self-inflicted, but authorities would not elaborate on who shot the three other victims. Police confirmed all four victims suffered gunshot wounds, and investigators are working to confirm the relationship between the victims. "Richmond police officers were on the scene within minutes after the call came in around 8:20 p.m. Wednesday.
