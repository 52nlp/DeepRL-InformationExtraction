1 killed, 3 injured in Early Morning Shooting - 6:30 PM UPDATE Posted: Sunday, January 5, 2014 9:38 AM EST Updated: Sunday, January 12, 2014 9:38 AM ESTShots were fired around 1 a.m. Sunday morning in an East Erie neighborhood, killing a 26-year-old Erie man and injuring another man and two women. The shooting occurred inside the East Erie Turners building at 829 Parade St., during a private party. "State police had to assist city police at UPMC Hamot when a large crowd gathered outside the Trauma Center, where the shooting victims were taken. Detectives tell us about 200 people were at the private party, but witnesses to the shooting are not being especially cooperative. The three wounded people - a 20-year-old man and women ages 18 and 19, were taken to UPMC Hamot with injuries that police said weren't life-threatening.
