Emergency responders rushed all four victims to Kings County Hospital, where they were in stable condition Sunday morning, officials said. Four men were shot after an argument erupted in Brooklyn Saturday night, police said. Police sources say authorities were searching for four teenage suspects, all dressed in black sweatshirts, who bolted down Lenox Rd. A 50-year-old man was shot in chest, two men ages 19 and 46 were both shot in the leg and another 19-year-old was shot in his foot in Prospect-Lefferts Gardens around 10:30 p.m., authorities said.
