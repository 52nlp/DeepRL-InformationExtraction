4 injured in party bus shooting (Photo: KARE)MINNEAPOLIS - Four people are injured after a shooting involving a party bus in downtown Minneapolis. Police spokesman John Elder tells the St. Paul Pioneer Press that all victims were "related or associated" with a party bus. Police say officers were called to a shooting at about 2:30 a.m. Saturday near 3rd Street North and Hennepin Avenue. Near 3rd Street and Nicollet Avenue, they found three adults who had gunshot wounds that were not life-threatening. Copyright 2014 The Associated Press.
