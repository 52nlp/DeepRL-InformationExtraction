Tampa police are investigating a shooting that injured five people early Saturday at the Supa D nightclub. In November 2013, one man died and another was injured in a shooting near the club. According to officers, unidentified people fired several shots after an argument broke out near the dance floor about 2 a.m.Two of those wounded drove themselves to area hospitals, and three were transported to hospitals by Tampa Fire Rescue, police said. Supa D, at 810 E. Skagway Ave., has been the site of two fatal shootings in the 13 months. Kenyatha Walker, a security worker at the club, was fatally shot in July.
