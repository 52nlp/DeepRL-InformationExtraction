One person was killed and three others were wounded in what police called a gang-related shooting in South Los Angeles on Saturday. Emergency responders at the scene of a shooting in South Los Angeles on Saturday, Dec. 27, 2014. Gunfire broke about 4:20 p.m. during a dispute between rival gang members in the area of Figueroa and 88th streets, according to the Los Angeles Police Department. A man and a woman were taken to the hospital, and one other man was treated at the scene and released. A 26-year-old man was pronounced dead at the scene, police said.
