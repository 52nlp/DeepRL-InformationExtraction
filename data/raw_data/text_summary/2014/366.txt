Four people were injured during an early morning shooting near the Mercedes-Benz Superdome, police said. By 5:33 a.m. police confirmed that four males were shot near Poydras and Bolivar streets and near Poydras Street and S. Claiborne Avenue. Police closed off the westbound lanes of traffic on Poydras Street from S. Claiborne Avenue to S. Galvez street during the investigation. One of the cars crashed at the intersection of Poydras and Bolivar streets, the report said. According to a report from WWL-TV, witnesses standing outside a nearby club said they saw people shooting at each other in two cars driving near the intersection of S. Roman and Perdido streets.
