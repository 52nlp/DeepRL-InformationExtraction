Oakland shooting kills 1, wounds 3 in FruitvaleA 36-year-old man was killed and three others were wounded Wednesday night in a shooting in Oakland's Fruitvale neighborhood, police said. The names of the victims weren't released. Police have not reported an arrest. The victims, two women and two men, were shot on 37th Avenue near International Boulevard about 9:50 p.m. One of the men died at a hospital, and the others, a 24-year-old man and two women, ages 18 and 26, are expected to survive.
