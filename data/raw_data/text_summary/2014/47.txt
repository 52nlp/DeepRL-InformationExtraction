At least one of the four people shot at a Stockton park Wednesday has died, police said. Three other people who were shot were taken to a hospital, but there is no word on their condition. Police are talking to neighbors to try and find any witnesses to the shooting. The shooting took place at Constitution Park on Fremont Street near Pilgrim Street at about 4:30 p.m.Watch report: 1 dead, 3 injured in Stockton shootingPolice said a 20-year-old man is dead, and the other three victims are a 17-year-old boy, an 18-year-old man and a 22-year-old man. Officers said this is a high crime neighborhood, and there has been gang activity in the area before.
