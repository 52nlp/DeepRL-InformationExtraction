Several shot at Las Vegas wedding reception Saturday Posted: Sunday, February 16, 2014 1:50 PM EST Updated: Sunday, April 27, 2014 2:31 PM EDTCrime scene tape surrounds the parking lot of a strip mall Feb. 16, 2014, after a shooting. A spokesman for Las Vegas Metro police at the scene told FOX5 that as many as five people were wounded in the shooting. The shooting happened about 11:30 p.m. at the El Palacio Del Sol banquet hall near the intersection of Sahara Avenue and Maryland Parkway.
