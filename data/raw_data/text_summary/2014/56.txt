DALLAS – A shooting at a Dallas nightclub left one man dead and six others injured early Sunday morning, reports CBS DFW. Police found Terrance Young shot outside CLUB B-N-H in Dallas. According to authorities, he is a suspect in the case and additional charges may follow pending investigation into Young’s death. Armed security at the club managed to halt the fight with the use of mace, but the violence spilled outside where shots were fired. Police arrested 22-year-old Eugene Rudd, who was jailed for unlawful possession of a weapon by a felon.
