Chief Hite briefs on shooting (WISH Photo/ Kevin Hankins)INDIANAPOLIS (WISH) – The Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department confirms four officers were shot on the southeast side Wednesday, and all are expected to be okay. Officers shot back, killing the suspect. Officials say four SWAT team officers were shot by the suspect’s gunfire. Two officers are expected to remain hospitalized overnight for observation and another officer has been released from the hospital.
