Man killed, 3 hurt in North Richmond shootingA man was killed and three others were hurt in a shooting in North Richmond, authorities said Sunday. One, a 23-year-old man, was taken by helicopter to John Muir Medical Center in Walnut Creek, where he died. Three other people, including two who arrived by private vehicle, suffered non-life-threatening wounds and were being treated at Doctors Medical Center in San Pablo. Deputies arrived and found two victims at the scene. The shooting happened in front of a home on the 1600 block of First Street about 10 p.m. Saturday, said Jimmy Lee, spokesman for the Contra Costa County Sheriff's Office, which patrols the unincorporated community.
