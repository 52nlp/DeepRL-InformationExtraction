Man Dies After Being Shot at West Garfield Park Party; Five Others Wounded View Full CaptionWEST GARFIELD PARK — A man is dead and five others are wounded after two gunmen opened fire on a party in a West Garfield Park home Saturday morning. • A 23-year-old man who was shot in his back shoulder and is in critical condition at Mount Sinai Hospital. • A 32-year-old man who was shot in his stomach and is in stable condition at Mount Sinai Hospital. • A 43-year-old man who was shot in his leg and is in good condition at Rush University Medical Center. • A 27-year-old woman who was shot in her foot and is in good condition at Mount Sinai.
