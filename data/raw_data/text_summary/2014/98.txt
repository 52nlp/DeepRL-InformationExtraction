PHOENIX - Police have identified a man killed when a shooting broke out between two neighborhood parties in Phoenix early Saturday morning. Two of the victims, ages 29 and 17, are in stable condition and the third victim, 33, is in critical, but stable condition, said police. Steve Martos said officers responded to a shooting call shortly after 2 a.m. near 55th Avenue and McDowell Road. One victim attempted to drive himself to the hospital, but was involved in a crash near 54th Avenue and Cypress. Police said two homes across the street from each other were hosting parties and at some point a fight broke out that led to gunfire.
