Davenport police are investigating an early morning shooting incident that left five people wounded. If anyone has information reference this incident they are encouraged to call the Davenport Police Department. Upon investigation officers located the scene of the shooting in the south alley of is 917 W 3rd St.A total of five individuals were struck. Police officers responded to both Genesis West and East campuses about 5:20 a.m., Friday, in response to multiple subjects with gunshot wounds. The incident is currently being investigated by the Detective Bureau.
