TUSKEGEE, Alabama -- A 22-year-old man was arrested in connection with the shooting that injured five people at an off-campus party near Tuskegee University on Jan. 11. According to reports, two men and two women were shot at a weekend party near the Tuskegee University bookstore. Tuskegee University is conducting its own investigation into the shooting. Ayden Harris, 19, a student at Tuskegee University was shot five times, WSFA reported. Three of the victims are Tuskegee University students.
