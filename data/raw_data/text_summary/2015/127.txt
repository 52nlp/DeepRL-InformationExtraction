Story highlights Police chief says the birthday party shooting was retaliationAuthorities say witnesses are not cooperating with police(CNN) A shooting at a child's birthday party that left one person dead and 11 wounded was a retaliation, Detroit Police Chief James Craig told WXYZ on Sunday. Four of the injured people are still hospitalized, Craig told WXYZ. Police think the shooting was retaliation, Craig said to WXYZ. Attackers fired semiautomatic weapons Saturday night at the party attended by about 300 people, police said. No children were shotPeople in the neighborhood are not helping officers find the shooters, Craig said.
