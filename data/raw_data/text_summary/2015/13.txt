Please enable Javascript to watch this videoPortsmouth, Va. - Five people were wounded in a shooting at Pure Diamonds Lounge in the 4100 block of Portsmouth Boulevard early Tuesday morning. Two men and a woman were being treated at Maryview Hospital for gunshots wounds. Police were called to the lounge around 2:00am for a large crowd and gunshots fired in the parking lot. Another man later walked into Maryview Hospital with a gunshot wound. One of those men was transported to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital for further treatment.
