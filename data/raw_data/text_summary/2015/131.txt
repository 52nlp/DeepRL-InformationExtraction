Investigators say the shooting is domestic and that they are not looking for a shooting suspect. According to deputies, Clifford Blanchard is a former St. Martin Parish Corrections Officer who last worked for the sheriff's office in 2004. Deputies say it happened just before 8 p.m. on La Rue Angelle near South Main Street in Breaux Bridge.
