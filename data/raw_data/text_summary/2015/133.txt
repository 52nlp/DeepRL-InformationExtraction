Violent night in New Orleans: Quadruple shooting in Central City reported shortly after Algiers shootingViolent night in New Orleans: Quadruple shooting in Central City reported shortly after Algiers shooting. Just after 10 p.m., New Orleans police reported a quadruple shooting in Central City. Two shooting incidents in New Orleans on Thursday night sent five victims to area hospitals, all with non-life threatening injuries. Soon after, NOPD reported another man was shot in Algiers in the 1000 block of Pacific Avenue. The conditions of the men injured in the triple shooting weren't released.
