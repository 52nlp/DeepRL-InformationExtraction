(WANE) – The Fort Wayne Police Department is investigating a shooting that left nine people injured on the city’s southeast side. FORT WAYNE, Ind. Anyone with information is asked to call the Fort Wayne Police Department at (260) 427-1222 or Crime Stoppers at (260) 436-7867. One woman was taken to a local hospital in critical condition, but she was later upgraded to serious condition. No weapons were recovered at the scene and police have not said what type of weapon might have been used in the shooting.
