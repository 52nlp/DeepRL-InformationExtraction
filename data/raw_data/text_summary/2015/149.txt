UPDATE: Gunmen Fatally Shot 3 People Near UMB Baltimore Campus From 2 VansGunmen got out of two vans and began firing at a group gathered on a corner Tuesday night, fatally shooting three people, police said. The two gunmen shot a total of four people - one who was in stable condition - a few blocks from the urban campus of the University of Maryland, Baltimore, according to police. "The three deaths bring the homicide total for Baltimore for the year to at least 154, according to police news releases. On Thursday, a passenger was injured when the rear window of a moving vehicle was shot. It was the second shooting in the block in a week, according to campus police.
