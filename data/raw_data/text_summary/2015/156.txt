Please enable Javascript to watch this videoCLEVELAND -- Cleveland police are investigating a drive-by shooting that injured several people Wednesday night. A 31-year-old male was shot in the thigh, and a 23-year-old male was shot in the hand and grazed on the hip. Police sources say at least seven people were hurt in the shooting, which happened at about 11 p.m. Wednesday in the 700 block of Thornhill. A 25-year-old male was shot in the knee, and a 21-year-old female was shot in the back. Police reports state that a 44-year-old female and a 21-year-old male were taken to MetroHealth Medical Center with gunshot wounds to their lower body.
