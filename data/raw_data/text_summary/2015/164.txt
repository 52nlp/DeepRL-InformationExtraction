Man Dead, Three Injured After Shooting in...One man is dead after a shooter shot three men in the Edgewood neighborhood of Northeast D.C. Friday night, police sources told News4's Shomari Stone. (Published Friday, July 17, 2015)One man is dead and three others wounded after a shooting Friday night in the Edgewood neighborhood of Northeast D.C., police said. Bryan Perkins, 18, of Northeast D.C., was pronounced dead after being shot. The shooting occurred about 9 p.m. on the 600 block of Edgewood Street NE, according to the Metropolitan Police Department. Three other men were also injured in the shooting.
