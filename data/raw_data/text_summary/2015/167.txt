LOUISVILLE, KY (WAVE) - Seven people, including a pregnant woman and her unborn baby, suffered what appeared to be non-life-threatening injuries in a shooting inside a Louisville nightclub early Sunday morning, Louisville Metro Police Department spokesman Dwight Mitchell said. The pregnant woman gave birth at University of Louisville hospital. All of them were taken to University of Louisville Hospital with non-life-threatening injuries. Witnesses told police a person believed to be a black male dressed in all white began shooting inside the club, hitting seven people. Previous reports that six people were shot were later clarified when WAVE 3 News learned the baby is considered the seventh victim.
