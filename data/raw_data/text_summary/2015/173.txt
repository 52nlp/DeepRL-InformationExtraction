Erie Teen Killed, 5 More Injured in Overnight Shooting Posted: Saturday, July 25, 2015 7:49 AM EDT Updated: Saturday, August 1, 2015 8:14 AM EDTERIE--- A rash of gunfire takes the life of a young teen and leaves five others injured in the city of Erie. Erie Police confirm that the terrible tragedy unfolded just before midnight in the 200 block of West 29th Street. Police could not confirm whether a rollover accident in the 2700 block of Myrtle Street overnight was related. One victim was lying in an alley behind a house on the corner of 29th and Summit Streets. Police tell us all of the victims are teenage males, with the deceased believed to be 16 or 17 years old.
