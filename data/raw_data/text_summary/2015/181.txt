A 22-year-old man was killed, and four others injured, in a shooting early Sunday in the city's North Austin neighborhood, officials said.No one is in custody. A 19-year-old was shot on the side and is in stable condition. A 20-year-old was shot in the stomach and is in stable condition. The two male shooters fired into a crowd, police said.The 22-year-old who died was shot in the head. The other victims are all males ages 19 to 24 years old.A 21-year-old man was shot in the back and chest and remains hospitalized in critical condition.
