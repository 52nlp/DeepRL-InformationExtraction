A woman was killed and three other people were injured when a fight escalated into a shooting Sunday night in Forest, authorities said. Forest police are interviewing witnesses, authorities said. The shooting was reported about 9:30 p.m. after the fight broke out during a large gathering at Poplar Avenue and Fern Street, police said. The names and conditions of those injured have not been released. At least one person was taken to the University of Mississippi Medical Center in Jackson.
