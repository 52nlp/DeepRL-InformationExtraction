BENNETTSVILLE, SC (WMBF) – Marlboro County Sheriff's deputies responded to a club shooting early Sunday morning August 16, involving five people who were shot, and several cut from broken glass. Marlboro County Sheriff’s Office deputies and investigators responded to the scene early Sunday and found that most people had left the club prior to arrival, according to MCSO. Sheriff Knight with the Marlboro County Sheriff's Office says, 19-year old Traquan D. Wall of Rockingham NC, was arrested for the shooting and taken into custody by the Rockingham Police Dept. The shooting took place at the State Line Club on Hwy 38N in Bennettsville. Those living nearby said having a club in the area solicits some crime, but nothing has ever cause great concern among residents.
