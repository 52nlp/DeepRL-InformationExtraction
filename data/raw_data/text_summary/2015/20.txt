Three people were hurt and a teenager died in a Stockton shooting Monday evening, police said. The three surviving victims, all hospitalized, have been identified as a 20-year-old man, an 18-year-old man and a 13-year-old boy, police said. It was one of the victim's homes where the shooting took place, officers said. A family member of two of the victims told KCRA 3 that his brother and cousin were among the injured. Gunfire rang out about 7 p.m. at a home in the 1500 block of East Ninth Street, near Airport Way, the Stockton Police Department said.
