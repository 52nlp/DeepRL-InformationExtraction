New Orleans police Tuesday evening (Aug. 25) were investigating the shooting death of a 19-year-old man at the intersection of Olive and Fern streets. In a shooting earlier Tuesday, a man and a toddler were wounded in the 7th Ward. Police have asked anyone with information about the shooting to call Homicide Detective Maggie McCourt at 504.658.5300 or Crimestoppers at 504.822.1111. Stay with NOLA.com | The Times-Picayune as more information becomes available on this breaking story. Witnesses told police a black Ford F-150 pickup sped away from the scene of the shooting.
