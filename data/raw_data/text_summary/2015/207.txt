UPDATE: SMITH COUNTY (KYTX) -- The Smith County Sheriff's Office has released surveillance video taken from the Bunny's Truck Stop following the fatal shooting of a Tyler man. "It's very possible some of the victims could be suspects as well," according to Smith County Larry Smith said. Anyone who witnessed the shooting or has information is urged to contact the Smith County Sheriff's Office at 903-590-2661. Smith County Sheriff Larry Smith said at least three people fired shots, and investigators found about 60 shell casings outside the truck stop. He said the video showed dozens of cars leaving the truck stop after the shooting.
