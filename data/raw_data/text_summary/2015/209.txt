(Aug. 30, 2015)MUSKEGON HEIGHTS, Mich. (WOOD) — Police in Muskegon Heights are investigating after four people were shot early Sunday. The scene of a shooting at East Park Manor in Muskegon Heights. Muskegon Heights police are investigating the motive behind the shooting. The shooting happened around 2:30 a.m. at East Park Manor in the 600 block of Hovey Street near Dyson Street. Four men in their late teens were shot, Matt Roberts, the chief trial attorney for the Muskegon County Prosecutor’s Office, confirmed to 24 Hour News 8.
