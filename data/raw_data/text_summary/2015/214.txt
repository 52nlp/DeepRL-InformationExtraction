Shooting scene (Photo: Scott Wright/KUSA)DENVER - Four people were hurt early Monday morning after multiple shots were fired in downtown Denver. Police say the shooting happened in a parking lot around 2:45 a.m. when a club let out. Investigators say the shooting happened near 19th and Blake streets. Market and Blake streets were closed between 18th and 19th streets for the investigation Monday morning. Two of them were treated and released, Denver Police say, and as of 3:30 p.m. Monday, 2 were still at the hospital with non-life threatening injuries.
