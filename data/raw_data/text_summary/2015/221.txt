Authorities are asking anyone with information about the crime to contact Crime Stoppers at (318) 673-7373 or visit their website at www.lockemup.org. Crime Stoppers is offering a reward for information leading to the arrest of those responsible for the crime. Just before 8:30 p.m. on September 12, 2015, Shreveport Police patrol officers were called to a home located the 1500 block of Clanton Street relative to a shooting in which multiple victims were injured. While no exact motive has been determined in the shooting, investigators do not believe the incident was a random act of violence. Preliminary information suggests the shooting took place after a dark colored sedan driving near the intersection of Clanton Street at Dowdell Street fired multiple gunshots from the moving car.
