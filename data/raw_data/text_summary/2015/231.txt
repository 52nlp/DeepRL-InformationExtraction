One person was killed while three others are hurt in a shooting inside a Philadelphia tavern that may be connected to a local motorcycle club. A 34-year-old man, who police say was wearing a "Wheels of Soul" jacket, was struck in the shooting. He was taken to Albert Einstein Hospital where he was pronounced dead at 6:44 p.m.A 53-year-old man, 42-year-old man and another 34-year-old man were also struck in the shooting. The victims were inside the Tangeray Tavern on the 900 block of Pratt Street early Sunday evening when a gunman opened fire.
