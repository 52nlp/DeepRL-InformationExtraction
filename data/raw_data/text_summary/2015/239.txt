Police said four people were shot outside a jazz club in the 18th & Vine District early Sunday. Officers said two groups of people exchanged gunfire outside the Juke House at 18th Street and Highland Avenue just before 3 a.m. A third victim went to a hospital in a private vehicle. Police said they've been talking to witnesses and examining dashboard camera video from officers who were in the area. A second person flagged down first responders near downtown Kansas City and was taken to a hospital.
