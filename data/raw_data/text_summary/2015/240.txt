Four shot outside jazz club in 18th & Vine District Posted: Sunday, September 27, 2015 10:02 AM EDT Updated: Tuesday, October 27, 2015 10:02 AM EDTPolice are investigating a quadruple shooting outside a jazz club in the 18th & Vine District early Sunday morning. Police are investigating a quadruple shooting outside a jazz club in the 18th & Vine District early Sunday morning. A third victim was driven to the hospital by another bar patron and also treated for a non-life threatening gunshot wound. When officers arrived on the scene, they found one victim lying on the sidewalk in front of the bar. He was shot in the upper torso and taken to the hospital in serious condition.
