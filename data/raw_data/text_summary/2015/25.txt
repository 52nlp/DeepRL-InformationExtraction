A barber is dead and three other men wounded after what police call a "vendetta" shooting in north Tulsa. It happened at the Gifted Hands Barber Shop at 1219 North Sheridan Road.Tulsa Police, fire and EMSA responded to the shooting around 9:00 Friday night.
