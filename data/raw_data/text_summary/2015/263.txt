NOPD: 4 women injured following Lower Ninth Ward shooting Updated: Monday, October 19, 2015 8:10 AM EDTNew Orleans police are investigating a shooting in the Lower Ninth Ward that left four women injured. Copyright 2015 WVUE. Officers initially said three women were injured in the shooting. The women were standing on a basketball court when shots were fired, according to the NOPD. If you have any information that could help police with this case, you are asked to call Crimestoppers at 504-822-1111.
