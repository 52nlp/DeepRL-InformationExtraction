Six hit in early morning Crockett County shooting Posted: Sunday, February 8, 2015 10:31 AM EST Updated: Sunday, February 8, 2015 11:10 PM ESTSix people were hit in a Crockett County shooting early Sunday morning. Crockett County Sheriff Troy Klyce says they have no suspects in custody, but they are still investigating the situation. According to the Crockett County Sheriff's Office, approximately 30 rounds were fired. One victim is in critical condition, and the other is stable. Copyright 2015 WMC Action News 5.
