(Photo: WFAA)Fort Worth police are investigating the shooting of five people in southwest Fort Worth on Tuesday evening. Five people were shot in the 1700 block of Southcrest Drive in Fort Worth on October 27, 2015. The victims were sprayed with shotgun pellets around 6 o'clock in the 1700 block of Southcrest Drive. Two who were struck refused treatment at the scene and three others were taken to John Peter Smith Hospital for treatment of minor injuries. All of the victims were adults; no one was seriously injured.
