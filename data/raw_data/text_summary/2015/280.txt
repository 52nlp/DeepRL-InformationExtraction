Please enable Javascript to watch this videoDES MOINES, Iowa - One person is dead and three others were injured following a shooting outside of a nightclub early Sunday morning. While investigating the shooting, the Polk County Sheriff's Office learned that three other gunshot wound victims had shown up at local hospitals. Shortly before 1:45 a.m., deputies from the Polk County Sheriff's Department responded to Evolution Nightclub, located at 4397 NW 6th Drive, on a report of a fight outside the club. When deputies arrived, they heard gunshots coming from an area around the club and located a man suffering from a gunshot wound. When police arrived, they found the body of Jose Lopez, 28, of Des Moines.
