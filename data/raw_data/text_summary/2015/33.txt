HOUSTON - Houston Police say three people are dead and two others injured in south Houston. Investigators say the shooting happened around 6:30 p.m. Wednesday night on Mallow Street near Noel Street. Investigators say they have seen drug activity in the area before, but no drugs or weapons were found inside the duplex. The two people transported to Methodist Hospital appeared to have gunshot wounds, but their condition is unknown. Detectives believe a group of men showed up to a duplex to rob a man when gunfire started.
