Orange County sheriff's deputies said they are searching for a gunman who shot four men in the Tangelo Park area. Investigators said there were three separate scenes in the Tangelo Park area connected to the shooting. After the shooting, everyone spread out to different parts of Tangelo to call 911," said Lt. Paul Hopkins of the Orange County Sheriff's Office . Officials said three of the shooting victims were brought to Orlando Regional Medical Center in serious condition, but two victims -- an 18 year old and a 19- year old -- have since been upgraded to critical condition. The shooting happened along Steyr Street shortly after 9 p.m. Sunday, deputies said.
