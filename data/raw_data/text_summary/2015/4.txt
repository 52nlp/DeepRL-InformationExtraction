(Published Wednesday, Jan. 7, 2015)One person was killed and three others were injured in a shooting in Miami Tuesday night, police said. Miami Police said the shooting happened on the 1300 block of NW 62nd Street in Miami. One of the shooting victims was in critical condition and later died, police said.
