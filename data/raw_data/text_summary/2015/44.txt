COMPTON (CBSLA.com) — Two female minors and two adult men reportedly were shot at a birthday party in Compton shortly after midnight Sunday. Deputies listed one of the victims as an 8-year-old girl and two of the men were said to be in their 20s. The four victims were transported to a hospital and all are expected to survive their injuries. Around 30 people, including women and children, were at a home in the 2700 block of West Alondra Boulevard when shots rang out around 12:15 a.m., the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department reported. A man was seen fleeing the scene but officials have not made any arrests.
