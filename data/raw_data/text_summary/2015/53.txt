Five injured at Lehigh Acres partyLEHIGH ACRES, Fla. – Five people were hospitalized after a fight broke out at a party in Lehigh Acres Saturday night, according to the Lee County Sheriff’s Office. The incident happened around 11:45 p.m. on 7th Street West. However, WINK News talked to the homeowner’s sister, who said the shooting happened during a 16th birthday party. “It was probably about 11:42 or something, I noticed a young fella that looked real strange,” said Lakesha Robinson. No arrest have been made in connection to what happened and the community is not in any danger.
