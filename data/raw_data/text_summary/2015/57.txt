PANAMA CITY BEACH, Fla. -- Seven people were injured, some critically, during an early-morning spray of gunfire Saturday at a spring break party on the Florida Panhandle, police said. The sheriff's office said several of the victims were students from Alabama A&M University in Panama City Beach for spring break. David Jamichael Daniels, 22, of Mobile, Alabama, was charged with seven counts of attempted murder and jailed awaiting a first court appearance. A seventh victim -- Anesia Powell, 20 -- was shot in the left arm, chest, and knee, and was undergoing surgery Saturday. A .40-caliber handgun believed to have been used was found in the yard of a nearby home.
