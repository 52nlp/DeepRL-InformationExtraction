A dispatch supervisor told WLWT News 5 that as far as police know now, only one gunman was involved. Police say a man was fatally shot and at least six other people were wounded at a residence in Dayton. Another person was found shot in the leg about a block away from the residence. Police said some victims were found at the scene, while others drove themselves to Miami Valley Hospital. WLWT News 5 will have more on this story as it becomes available.
