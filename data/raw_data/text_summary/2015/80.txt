BUFFALO, N.Y. (WIVB) — Buffalo Police Officers responded Monday evening to a quadruple shooting on Roosevelt Avenue on the city’s east side. Police arrived to the scene of the serious shooting just before 5:30 p.m. on the first block of Roosevelt Avenue. Another 17-year-old male remains at ECMC Tuesday morning listed in good condition. The shooting appears to be gang related, according to Buffalo Police. A 17-year-old male was treated and released from ECMC.
