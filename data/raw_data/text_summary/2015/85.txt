WASHINGTON – One person is dead, and four others injured, after a Tuesday night shooting in Prince George’s County, Maryland. Police say five male victims were taken to the hospital, and one of the victims died. It happened around 11:15 p.m. along Pard Road and Glacier Avenue in Capitol Heights. They are investigating the shooting as a homicide.
