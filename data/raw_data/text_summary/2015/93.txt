Updated with more details at 11:48 a.m.Four people were shot during an attack Sunday night in the 2700 block of Dayton Street in the Jeff-Vander-Lou neighborhood of St. Louis. Children belonging to two of the victims were inside of the apartment at the time of the attack and were unharmed. The victims were taken to an area hospital and are all in stable condition. Officers called to the scene found the victims lying in the entrance to an apartment. They are now in the custody of family members, police said.
