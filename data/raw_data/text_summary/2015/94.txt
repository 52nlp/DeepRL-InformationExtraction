4th suspect charged in connection to May 24 Smiley Court shooting Posted: Sunday, May 24, 2015 11:02 PM EDT Updated: Sunday, September 13, 2015 11:43 PM EDTThe Montgomery Police Department has charged a fourth suspect in connection to the May 24 shooting on East Smiley Court that left one person dead and three others injured. According to jail records, Ladarren Seawright, 16, is charged with reckless murder. Adrian Banks, Olando Harris and Jezereel Pett, all 23 and from Montgomery, were arrested in June and charged with one count each of reckless murder. According to court documents, gunfire was exchanged in a large crowd between several people in "rival groups. Seawright and three others are accused of killing 18-year-old Rodriquez Smith, of Montgomery.
