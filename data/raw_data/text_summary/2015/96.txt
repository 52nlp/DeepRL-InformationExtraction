Police responded to a report of gunfire about 1:30 a.m., outside of Dino's Bar on 1002 Main St., Brockton Police Lt. Kenneth LaGrice said. Five men were shot in Brockton early Sunday as a bar crowd was let out for the night, our news partners at the Brockton Enterprise report. It is not known if he was inside Dino's before the shooting outside, LaGrice said. Five males were hit by bullets in the incident, two victims are from Brockton and two from Boston, according to LaGrice. This is not the first shooting incident at Dino's.
