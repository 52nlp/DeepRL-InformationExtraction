Six people were shot in a Huntington, West Virginia, housing complex on Tuesday night, local media reported.  WSAZ-TV reported that they were shot at the Northcott Court housing complex.  Five of the victims are being treated at local hospitals.  Authorities found a firearm and a magazine, but the suspect is at large.  The Charleston Gazette reported that three people walked into Cabell-Huntington Hospital with gunshot wounds.  Local police are investigating the matter.  Initial reports said they were shot at a gas station, but that is unconfirmed.
