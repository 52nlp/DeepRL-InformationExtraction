Stock photo (Photo: Getty Images/iStockphoto)  Four men were shot near Lewis Street in Rochester early Saturday.  Rochester police responded at 4:09 a.m. to 188 Lewis St. after a 911 caller reported a man shot, Rochester Police Department Capt. Michael Callari said in a news release.  At that time, Rochester General Hospital reported receiving four gunshot victims who arrived in private vehicles. Rickey Harvey,29, Patrick Carter, 34, Joel Allen and Darelle Scott, 26, were were shot at least once in the upper body. None of the injuries were life-threatening, Callari said.  Details of the incident are unknown. The investigation is ongoing.  Read or Share this story: http://on.rocne.ws/1bWkTWC
